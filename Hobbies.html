My Hobbies
Running
I've been running for about 4 years now and it's something that I find joy in. It's important to me because it has been a big part of my life from middle to highschool. I spend a lot of time getting better in practice, out of practice, and during my off seasons. I've also made many cherishable friends because of it.
Lifting
Lifting is another hobby I enjoy. I first started lifting when I was 12 and have grown to love it over the years. It's important to me because it's a way for me to spend time with my father and brother and make new, lasting memories with them. We also go out to eat after and spend more time together. In addition, it acts as a therapy for me, helping me get rid of some stress and improving my mood.
Going Out
Going out with friends and family is also very important to me because it's fun, I get to try new foods, and I get to make more memories with them. They are the people I can go to for help and to have fun so being able to go out with them is important. I also like to eat good food, which adds to the joy.
